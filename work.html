Getting Started

Own your dream house in four easy guided steps

1. Find property

Search our listing to find your preferred home type or use the simple form provided to tell us your preferences.

3. Move in

Pay the deposit amount and move in. You can pay the agreed balance as monthly, quarterly or annual instalments.

2. Make offer

Select the most convenient payment plan from the options available or create your own and send your offer to us.

4. Own it

Collect your home documents at the end of the contract period or when you have completed the agreed payment, whichever is earlier.
